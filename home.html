Welcome!

Unmanned Aerial Gators is the premier drone design team for the University of Florida. Utilizing the technical knowledge and skills of members, UAG works to achieve feats and overcome challenges set by the IARC. Working together, our group hopes to surpass other universities and reach goals that other drones have not accomplished before.
